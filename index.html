About

Battle begins. The player and enemy have on 100hp. The player chooses the spell first. The choice of spell is carried out by means of a modal dialogue. The title and subsequent animation of spells. The player goes to the task screen. In the case of a successful decision - the spell is used, otherwise the transition speed towards the monster. Monster uses a spell, deals damage to the player. If the player won the battle than goes to the next monster. If you lose - you will see a table of records, with the number of monsters you defeated and the name of the hero which you choose.
